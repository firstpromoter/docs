---
title: "Braintree"
description: "Connecting FirstPromoter with Braintree"
---

`Difficulty: Easy` <br/>
`Developer Required: No`

FirstPromoter enables you to automatically track sales and cancellations by connecting your Braintree account.

<Note>
Refund tracking is not currently supported for Braintree. Only successful subscription charges and cancellations are tracked.
</Note>

**Tracked webhook events:**

| Braintree Event | What FirstPromoter tracks |
|---|---|
| `SubscriptionChargedSuccessfully` | Sale / commission |
| `SubscriptionCanceled` | Cancellation |

Click [here](https://login.firstpromoter.com?redirect=%2Fintegration%2Fbraintree) to view the setup guide.
