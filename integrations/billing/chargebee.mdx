title: "Chargebee"
description: "Connecting FirstPromoter with Chargebee"
---

`Difficulty: Easy` <br/>
`Developer Required: No`

FirstPromoter enables you to automatically track sales, refunds, and cancellations by connecting your Chargebee account.

**Tracked webhook events:**

| Chargebee Event | What FirstPromoter tracks |
|---|---|
| `customer_created` | Signup (only when a tracking ID or referral ID is present) |
| `subscription_created` | Signup (including when a 100% discount is applied and no payment event fires) |
| `payment_succeeded` | Sale / commission |
| `payment_refunded` | Refund |
| `subscription_cancelled` | Cancellation |

Click [here](https://login.firstpromoter.com?redirect=%2Fintegration%2Fchargebee) to view the setup guide.
