`Difficulty: Intermediate` <br/>
`Developer Required: Yes`

<Note>
Depending on the complexity of your website it is ideal to have a developer assist you in the setup.
</Note>

If you are using the paddle checkout feature on your website you can integrate with FirstPromoter.
This setup mainly requires you to add tracking scripts into your website and setting extra configurations on your paddle billing/classic checkout function `Paddle.Checkout.open()` or `Paddle.Initialize()`.

Click [here](https://login.firstpromoter.com?redirect=%2Finstructions%2Fpaddle-checkout) to view the guide.
