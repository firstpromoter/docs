---
title: "New Customer"
---
By setting up a webhook your endpoint will receive an event when a lead becomes a customer.
<Note>
This event is triggered only once on the first payment made by the lead.
</Note>

## Response Paramerters

<ResponseField name="Event" type="Event Object">
  <Expandable title="properties">
    <ResponseField name="id" type="string">
      The Id of the event
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="type" type="string">
      Indicates the webhook event type, such as `lead_becomes_referral`.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="created_at" type="string">
      Timestamp of when the event occurred.
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>
